Questions

The stuff people actually ask.

What exactly do I get for $500?

A complete, custom website built for your business: mobile-first, fast, with your services, photos, reviews, tap-to-call, and a contact or booking flow. It’s built to show up on Google for local searches, and you get two rounds of revisions before it goes live.

What do the monthly plans cover?

The Care Plan ($99/mo) covers hosting, security, backups, edits when your business changes, weekly checks, and a short monthly report. The Premium Care Plan ($150/mo) adds everything in that plan plus Google Business Profile management and local SEO to help you rank in nearby searches. Both are month to month, and you can cancel anytime.

What’s your refund policy?

No. All fees are non-refundable. That’s why the build includes two rounds of revisions and we get everything right together before it goes live. If you cancel the care plan, billing simply stops for the next month.

I already have a website. Why switch?

If it’s slow, hard to use on a phone, or not bringing you business, it’s costing you quietly. I’ll look at it for free and tell you straight whether it’s worth rebuilding.

Do you only work with certain businesses?

No. Barbershop, lawn care, auto shop, and more. If you’re a local business that wants more calls and bookings, I can build for you. Take a look at the builds above.
