The Shop

Not your average chair.

Crown opened in the River Market in 2015 with one idea: give a real barbershop the polish it deserves. Marble, brass, good music, and blades that stay sharp.

Ten years and thousands of cuts later, the coffee's hot, the fades are cleaner than ever, and there's a chair with your name on it. Come in a walk-in, leave a regular.
