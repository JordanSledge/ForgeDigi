Our Story

The fire, and the table around it.

Ember & Oak began with a wood-burning hearth and a simple idea: take the Southern food we grew up on and give it the room, the sourcing, and the care of a proper kitchen.

Everything touches the fire. We break down whole animals, dry-age our own beef, and change the menu with what the farms and the Gulf bring in. Come hungry, and stay late.
