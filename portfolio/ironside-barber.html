The Shop

A real barbershop, not a franchise.

Ironside opened on Dickson Street in 1996 with two chairs, a jar of pomade, and a rule that still stands: nobody leaves until it looks right.

Thirty years later we are five chairs deep, the coffee is hot, the clippers are sharp, and the conversation is free. Come in a regular, leave a regular.
