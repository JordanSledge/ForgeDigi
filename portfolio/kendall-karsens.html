Our Story

From boardroom to kitchen.

Kendall Karsen's is named for Chef Kevin Winston's two sons. After years in the corporate world, and a doctorate to show for it, he traded the desk for the kitchen and the soul food he was raised on.

Everything is cooked to order and made fresh, which is exactly why it sells out. Some Sundays the yams are gone by two o'clock. Upscale soul food, no shortcuts.

Chef Kevin Winston, Owner
