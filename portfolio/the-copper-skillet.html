Our Story

Three generations, one cast-iron skillet.

It started with Miss Ruby's kitchen and a copper skillet she swore made everything taste better. Fifty years later, that same skillet still hangs by the pass, and every plate that leaves our kitchen carries a little of her Sunday-supper spirit.

We buy from Arkansas farms, smoke our own meats, and bake the cornbread fresh for every service. Nothing here is in a hurry, and that's exactly the point.
