Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026 · Forge Digital · Conway, Arkansas

How Forge Digital handles your information, in plain language. This is not legal advice. Have an attorney review it before you rely on it.

Forge Digital, owned by Jordan Sledge in Conway, Arkansas, respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and what we do with it.

What we collect

  • Info you send us: when you email, call, text, or book a call, we receive what you share, such as your name, phone number, email, business name, and message.
  • Payment info: payments are handled by Stripe. Stripe processes your card details directly. We do not see or store your full card number.
  • Scheduling: if you book a call through a scheduling tool, that tool receives the details you enter to set up the appointment.
  • Basic site data: like most websites, we may collect basic, non-identifying usage data (such as pages visited) to understand how the site is used.

How we use it

  • To reply to you and talk about your project.
  • To design, build, host, and maintain your website.
  • To send invoices and process payments.

How we share it

We do not sell your information. We share it only with the service providers we use to run the business, such as Stripe for payments, a scheduling tool for booking calls, and an email provider for messages. Each handles your data under its own privacy terms. We may also share information if required by law.

Cookies

Our site may use minimal cookies or similar tools for basic functionality and usage measurement. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we have about you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing jordan.forgedigital@gmail.com. You can opt out of any of our messages at any time.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. No method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we can’t guarantee absolute security.

Children

Our services are meant for businesses and adults. We don’t knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes

We may update this policy. The current version will always be posted here.
